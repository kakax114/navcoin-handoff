Protocol Proposal

NPIP001: Static Block Reward

50%

Craig MacGregor

After the ClockSync fix was soft forked into the network a couple of months ago, NavCoin is now compliant with the Proof of Stake v2 protocol as published by Blackcoin: https://blackcoin.org/blackcoin-pos-protocol-v2-whitepaper.pdf The next logical step is to become compliant with PoS v3. The spec can be read here: https://bravenewcoin.com/assets/Whitepapers/Blackcoin-POS-3.pdf The short version is that PoS v3 includes cold staking capability and a fixed block reward. We have already presented cold staking in NPIP002 and it has received unanimous support from the community.
